The Crystal Gems are captured!

While exploring some Gem ruins, Steven, Garnet, Amethyst, and Pearl have been separated, and have a limited time to escape! In front of each of them is a button.

Garnet's future vision shows her that pressing any button adds a number of stones to a pulley's counterweight, and only an exact number of stones will open the door. Any more, and the mechanism will collapse and trap them forever!

Garnet can see the weight needed to open the door, but cannot determine the number of stones that a button will release.

Can you get the Crystal Gems to safety?
